Why I'd Like to be a Part of Founders and Coders

I was a secondary science teacher for four years. Kids are great but exhausting, so I knew I’d have to do something else. I’ve always had a passion for tech and problem solving and reasoned that I could start to learn the dark arts coding whilst traveling around Asia for a few months. That time gave me a foundation, but I have a lot more to learn.

Founders and Coders is particularly appealing because of the focus on community. I’ve been an educator and understand the value of helping people achieve. The opportunity to learn in an environment where that's appreciated is one I’d be grateful to have. Having the chance to help others learn the same skills afterwards would be even better.
